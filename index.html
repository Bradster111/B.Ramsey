SHEET 02 — PROFILE

About

FIG. 1 — B. RAMSEY

I'm a mechanical engineer who likes seeing a project through — from a rough sketch to a machine that runs a production line. At Rice, I focused on mechanical design and prototype manufacturing, paired with a business minor to keep the commercial and management side of engineering decisions in view.

Since graduating, I've led capital projects for packaging and mixing systems. Before graduating, I engineered precision fluid-handling hardware for a biomedical application, built forecasting models for energy markets, and worked on pipeline & power engineering problems in the field. I'm most interested in roles where I can own hardware design or program execution end-to-end.

Outside of work I enjoy weightlifting, camping and hiking, 3D printing, tinkering, and cooking.

I have also had the honor and privilege to receive the following awards: Eagle Scout, Vigil Honor, QuestBridge Scholar.
