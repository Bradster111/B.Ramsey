EAGLE SCOUT
PROJECT

Before any of my formal engineering training, my Eagle Scout project was the first thing I planned and led from proposal to finished build.

That meant scoping the project, getting it approved, organizing materials and a volunteer crew, and managing the schedule and budget through to completion — project management fundamentals I didn't have a name for yet, but that show up directly in how I run engineering projects today.

Completing the project earned me the rank of Eagle Scout, along with the Vigil Honor for continued service within the Order of the Arrow.
